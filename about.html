Photographer based in Berlin and Brussels, stills and motion projects for brands, people and publications worldwide.

„Lars’ photography is defined by a playful, human, and cinematic energy that brings personality to every frame. Moving between staged and documentary approaches, he uses portraiture, fashion, and everyday scenes to explore contemporary identity, culture, beauty, and place. Driven by a belief in photography as an inclusive force for positive change, his work seeks to unite and spark connection. His warm, organic images and grounded sensibility illuminate the evolving relationship between people and their environment.“ Oliveriero Toscani
